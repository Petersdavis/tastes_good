2. Partnership

  1. Tastes-Good.com partners with restaurants across Canada with the intent to promote their menu's online. Our services include processing orders online, safeguarding order and restaurant related data, printing orders in the restaurant, generating financial reports, providing tools to change the restaurants menu and to generate coupons, processing payments online (depending on a third party payment processor). Our partnerships depend upon the ongoing mutual consent of both parties. Both the restaurants and Tastes-Good.com reserve the right to terminate the partnership for any reason without additional fee by written or electronic notification. It is expected that the termination of services should be concluded within 7 days of such notification, and that within 31 days of termination outstanding accounts should be resolved.

3. Restaurant Responsibilities

  1. The restaurant agrees to ensure that their menu is up to date--that all products are available and that pricing is accurate.
  2. The restaurant agrees to ensure that the restaurant information including telephone number, email address and schedule is accurate and up to date.
  3. The restaurant agrees to guarentee the quality and completeness of every order and to deliver the orders to the customer in a timely fashion.
  4. If the restaurant is unable to complete an order it is their responsibility to communicate this fact to the customer and to cancel the order in a timely fashion

4. Tastes-Good.com Responsibilities

  1. Tastes-Good.com is responsible to ensure that the webserver is constantly accessable.
  2. Tastes-Good.com is responsible to keep records of every transaction and to make these records available to the restaurant partner.
  3. Tastes-Good.com is responsible for ensuring that the restaurant is made aware of and confirms or rejects every order--either by client side software, by email, or by telephone
  4. Tastes-Good.com is responsible for the security of the website interface so that users cannot access data without authorization and that users cannot modify the prices or descriptions of items
  5. Tastes-Good.com is responsible for tracking customer complaints and reserves the right to blacklist customers who are serial abusers of our refunds policy.

5. Settlement of Accounts

  1. Accounts will be settled when the net amount pending settlement is greater than $100 or at the termination of the partnership
  2. Accounts with balences greater than $100 are settled bi-weekly by email payment through pay-pal.
  3. Accounts may be settled by check or bank transfer although in these cases the restaurant will be charged a processing fee to cover the cost of settlement

6. Refunds and Store Credit

  1. If a customer complains that an item is missing from an order, the restaurant will refund the the value of the item to customer directly if the order is paid in store or upon delivery; or through our web-interface, if the order is paid online; or to deliver the item to the customer.
  2. If a customer is dissatisfied with the quality of an item that is part of an order, the restaurant agrees to offer the customer store-credit at equal to or greater than the value of the item.
  3. These requirements are guidelines for settling complaints. The restaurant may offer other forms of compensation provided that there is mutual agreement between the restaurant and the customer
  4. If the restaurant believes that a customer is pursuing a complaint dishonestly they may refer the complainent to www.Tastes-Good.com for arbitration.
  5. If the customer lodges a complaint with www.Tastes-Good.com and the order was payed online. www.Tastes-Good.com reserves the right to refund the order unilaterally depending on the circumstances of the case, the customers order and complaint history.
  6. Processing Payment of Refunds
    • Order Paid Online:
      • Tastes-Good.com is solely responsible for processing refunds of all payments made online.
      • If the restaurant partner wishes to issue a refund for an order that is paid online they must contact us and we will issue the refund on their behalf.
      • The restaurant partner must not make any payment to the customer directly. If the restaurant partner does issue a refund directly to the customer Tastes-Good.com is not liable if the customer also receives a refund of the transaction through our online-interface.
    • Order Paid In-Store or Upon Delivery:
      • Tastes-Good.com is not responsible for processing any refunds of orders that are not payed through our online-interface.
      • The restaurant partner may use our online-interface to email their customer a store credit (or) they may offer the customer a refund in cash (or) they may offer store-credit to the customer directly

7. Orders Paid Online

  1. If payment is received for an order online the funds are withheld for a minimum of 72 hours.
  2. If the customer makes a complaint against a restaurant for refusing to offer a refund or store credit, Tastes-Good.com reserves the right to cancel the order and to refund payment to the customer.
  3. If an order is cancelled the restaurant is not charged commission on the order and the restaurant does not receive payment for the order.
  4. In making a decision to cancel an order we will investigate the customers transaction history and previous complaints.

8. Liability

  1. The liability of Tastes-Good.com for a particular order is limited to the commission charges associated with the order.
  2. The restaurants liability is limited to the remainder of the order total after commissions are deducted.
  3. If a credit card was used fraudulently to place an order, Tastes-Good.com is liable for chargeback fees incured.
  4. If a credit card was used fraudulently to place an order, the restaurant partner is liable for the cost of refunding to the credit card holder.
